RagnaCustoms Redesign

Case Study

Overview

RagnaCustoms, an online platform to download, share, discover, and rate custom songs for Ragnarock VR, was in dire need of a redesign to help improve its usability and visual design. I approached the team to offer my services in January 2022 with an eye on their anniversary date to launch V2 in April.

My redesign took inspiration from popular music and community websites to promote user activity, daily visits, and community engagement.

Role & Scope

As the User Experience Designer, I researched, ideated, and prototyped the redesign for Ragnacustoms.com. Currently, I still play an active role in shaping the experience on the site.

This case study is undergoing some finishing touches and will be available by July 22nd. Want to learn more before then? Please reach out on LinkedIn or E-mail! You can view my other case studies on my work page.
