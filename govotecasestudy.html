Go Vote

Case Study

Project Overview

A proposed federal voting app for US Citizens that allows citizens to learn about upcoming elections, register to vote, research candidates, and submit their ballot electronically.

Role & Scope

This platform was a team project for my UX/UI certification where I handled research, ideation, design, testing, and prototyping. The project’s scope focused on user onboarding and reviewing a city’s music offerings.

Research & Definition

Interview Insights

Through our user interviews, we learned that potential voters do not like waiting in line, often don't find time to vote, and are slighlty skeptical of online voting in terms of security.

Affinity Diagram

Story Board

Competitor Analysis

Due to the federal constraints on voting online, we had to get creative in a competitive analysis. Estonia uses an all-online voting platform with great success while there is a competitor that offers online voting in the US -- until security issues has sidelined the project.

Ideation

I Like / I Wish / What If

Brainstorming the features to include in an app that potentially every US Citizen with a phone would have access to was a liberating process.

Feature Prioritization Matrix

At the same time, we had to be realistic and refer back to our user research to remain grounded.

Our Solution to Simplify

Registration

Registering to vote is registering for the app.

Election Information

Candidate overviews, election notifications, and polling place information helps inform citizens.

Secure Ballot

Easy to fill out ballots with paper receipts -- and a selfie filter! -- help drive voter turnout and keep our elections secure.
